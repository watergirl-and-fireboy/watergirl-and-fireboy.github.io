Core Controls and Mechanics

Keyboard Layout

Water child uses the W A S D keys for movement and jumps. Fire child answers with the arrow keys. Press R to restart a room at any time. On mobile you tap arrow buttons and a character-swap circle.

Element Rules

Fire child swims in lava but dissolves in water. Water child swims in water but evaporates in lava. Neither hero survives a single drop of toxic green fluid, so plan every leap with care.

Level Categories Inside Forest Temple

Normal Chambers

Seventeen standard stages introduce conveyor belts, seesaw lifts, sliding boxes, and pressure plates. Your mission is to collect gems in any order then race both heroes toward the twin exits.

Simultaneous Designed Chambers

Six mirrored arenas push perfect synchronization. Both halves share identical layouts, so players must mirror jumps, flip twin switches, and time door crossings together.

Green or White Diamond Chambers

Nine special rooms remove ordinary gems and focus on one bright jewel. Often a single risky jump decides success, and one mistimed landing forces a complete restart.

Essential Tips for Rank A Completion

Study Before You Step

Before touching a key, scan the entire map. Note elevators, rotating gears, and every hazard. A ten-second preview saves countless resets later.

Use Teamwork Even Solo

Controlling both heroes alone? Park one on a switch, move the other to a safe spot, then swap. Small cycles build steady progress and reduce panic jumps.

Avoid the Green Goo at All Costs

The green fluid eliminates either hero instantly. Do not test, do not touch—plan jumps that clear its surface with room to spare.

Reset Fast, Learn Faster

Press R immediately after a mistake. Instant resets turn failure into rapid muscle-memory training, letting you shave seconds from your best clear times.
